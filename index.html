How It Works

🌍
360° Video Scenes

Each location is a real 360° video filmed on campus. Look around freely using your mouse or phone gyroscope.

💬
Live Captions

Every scene has a timed voiceover with matching captions that appear and fade as you explore — great for accessibility.

🏷️
AR Annotations

Labelled images and directional arrows appear at the right moments, pointing out key areas and guiding you through each location.

🎥
Scene Switcher

Press Q (or the controller button in VR) to bring up the scene selection panel and jump between all five locations instantly.

🦹
VR Headset Ready

Fully supports Quest and other WebXR headsets for a proper immersive experience, as well as desktop and mobile browsers.

🔊
Voiceover Audio

Each scene has its own narration track that plays automatically and syncs with the captions and on-screen annotations.
